### Play
**Step-by-step gameplay:**

#### PART 1: First Run - Player Choice
1. **Character Selection:**  
   - Player taps one of three characters (Maya, Diego, or Zara)  
   - Selected character steps forward  
   - Brief intro: "Maya notices colors and patterns best!" (or appropriate for selected character)  
   - "Ready to Run!" button appears  

2. **Running Begins:**  
   - Memory Lane appears in 3D perspective (path scrolling toward player)  
   - Character automatically runs forward at steady pace  
   - Path has three lanes (left, center, right)  
   - Player swipes left/right to change lanes (or arrow keys)  
   - Simple jump mechanic: swipe up/tap to jump over obstacles  

3. **Memory Fragments Appear:**  
   - Glowing memory orbs float above the path  
   - Three types based on what happened at "the school field trip":  
     - VISUAL fragments (colors, sights): Yellow orbs with eye icon  
     - ACTION fragments (movement, activities): Green orbs with running icon  
     - SOCIAL fragments (people, interactions): Blue orbs with face icon  
   - Fragments appear mixed across all three lanes  
   - Character must be in correct lane to collect (run through orb)  

4. **Character Strengths:**  
   - Based on selected character, certain fragments glow brighter:  
     - Maya: Visual fragments glow brightest (easiest to see and collect)  
     - Diego: Action fragments glow brightest  
     - Zara: Social fragments glow brightest  
   - Dimmer fragments can still be collected but require more attention/skill  
   - This simulates: we naturally notice what interests us most  

5. **Forgetting Fog Obstacles:**  
   - Gray fog clouds drift across lanes  
   - Cannot be avoided completely - will touch some  
   - When character runs through fog:  
     - Screen briefly becomes slightly hazy  
     - One collected fragment (if any) fades from collection bag  
     - Represents normal forgetting over time  
   - Not a "failure" - game text says: "Memory naturally forgets some things!"  

6. **Collection Choices:**  
   - Player can only collect fragments they actively run toward  
   - Path scrolls quickly - must make split-second decisions  
   - Can't collect everything (too many fragments in too many lanes)  
   - Must choose: "Which fragments matter to ME?"  
   - This simulates selective attention and encoding  

7. **Distance Markers:**  
   - Path has markers every 100 meters:  
     - "100m: Arrived at museum"  
     - "200m: Saw dinosaur bones"  
     - "300m: Ate lunch in park"  
     - "400m: Played on playground"

   - After 500 meters (approximately 90 seconds of running)
